惜食堂：Food Angel

Food Angel is a non-profit organization based in Hong Kong, established by the renowned charity Bo Charity Foundation in 2011. Its mission is to reduce food waste, alleviate hunger, support vulnerable groups in the community, and promote environmental conservation and sustainability.

Through its food rescue and assistance programs, Food Angel collects unsold or near-expired but still safe and nutritious food. The food is then processed and cooked into nutritious meals and distributed to low-income families, the elderly, schoolchildren, and other people in need.

Food Angel’s operations are supported by volunteers, food donors, and sponsors. The organization operates several community kitchens and food distribution centers across Hong Kong.

Address: 辦公室. 九龍深水埗海壇街235號豐盛大廈2樓

Email: info@foodangel.org.hk

Phone: +852 2801 5333

雅麗氏何妙齡那打素慈善基金會

Alice Ho Miu Ling Nethersole Charity Foundation

Intro: 長者院舍、院牧服務、外展復康事工、全人健康教育工作、癌症病人及家庭支援服務等

The Alice Ho Miu Ling Nethersole Charitable Foundation was founded in 1887. It is a Christian organization whose service purposes are: 1) Care, treatment, and comfort (in body, mind, and spirit) for the weak, sick, and elderly or those in need of assistance; 2) Promote and educate the community on health care and disease prevention; 3) Train doctors, nurses, midwives, and other allied health staff; and 4) Demonstrate and spread the Christian faith through services so that people can get help for body, mind, and spirit.
